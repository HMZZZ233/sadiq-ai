Why Hamzah Build Sadiq?

The Vision

Sadiq AI was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create an AI assistant that truly understands and helps people in their daily lives. We wanted to build something more than just a chatbot ‚Äì we envisioned an intelligent companion that could adapt, learn, and grow with its users.

The Mission
